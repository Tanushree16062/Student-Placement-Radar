---

## 📌 Overview

When entering the final year of college, the biggest question on a student's mind is: *"Am I statistically on track to get placed?"* 

This project attempts to answer that. By feeding historical student data into a **Random Forest Classifier**, this model identifies the underlying patterns of successful candidates and outputs an immediate binary prediction (`Placed` / `Not Placed`). 

**The Goal:** To give students an early reality check, and give college placement cells a tool to identify "at-risk" students who might need extra interview preparation.

---

* **Data Cleaning & Manipulation:** `Pandas`, `NumPy`
* **Machine Learning:** `Scikit-Learn` *(RandomForestClassifier, StandardScaler)*
* **Development Environment:** Google Colab
